Big Batch Mai Tai

Description

A Mai Tai is a fruity cocktail perfect for summer parties! /h2>

Ingredients

  • 1 C light rum
  • 1 C Jamaican rum
  • 1 C orange liquor
  • 1/2 C lime juice
  • 1/2 C orange juice
  • 1/2 C orgeat syrup
  • grenadine
  • lime wheels
  • orange wheels
  • marischino cherries

Instructions

  1. Fill large pitcher or drink dispenser with ice
  2. Pour liquid ingredients except for grenadine over ice
  3. When serving the cocktail gently pour grenadine in thin layer over the top
  4. Garnish with lime and orange wheels and top with a cherry!
